⚠️ Key Success Factors & Considerations

🎯
Scope Definition: A clear GAP/ROI Analysis before implementation reduces scope creep and protects ROI. This is why Nexa Solutions runs a structured ROI workshop as the first project phase — 80% of clients who complete it convert to full projects.
👥
Change Management: User adoption is the #1 ROI killer. Dedicated training, key-user programs, and post-go-live hypercare ensure the system is actually used — translating theoretical savings into real results.
🔄
Data Migration Quality: Clean historical data migration is critical. Budget adequate time for cleansing master data (customers, vendors, products) before go-live to avoid operational disruption.
📐
Customisation Balance: Minimise custom development. Odoo's out-of-the-box functionality covers 80%+ of standard business processes. Excessive customisation inflates costs and complicates future upgrades.
